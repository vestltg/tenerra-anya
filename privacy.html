The short version: This is a marketing website. We do not collect personal data on this site. If you consent, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. That's it.

This Privacy Policy applies only to this marketing website at anya.tenerra.ai. It describes the limited data practices for this site only.

1. Who We Are

Vest Life, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") is a Delaware corporation operating this marketing website at anya.tenerra.ai under the brand Tenerra.ai. We build continuity infrastructure for families of children with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD).

For privacy questions, contact us at support@vestlife.com.

2. What We Collect

This is a marketing website. We do not offer user accounts, login, subscription management, billing, forms, uploads, or on-site content storage. We do not collect your name, email address, or payment information through this site.

  • Analytics data (with consent): If you consent via our cookie banner, we collect anonymized usage data through Google Analytics (GA4) — pages visited, session duration, and general interaction patterns. No personally identifiable information is passed to Google Analytics.

3. Cookies and Tracking

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Only loaded with your explicit consent via our cookie banner. You can opt out at any time by clicking "Decline" on the banner or clearing your browser's localStorage (key: ga_consent). You can also install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We use localStorage only to remember your cookie consent choice so we do not show the banner repeatedly. We do not use localStorage for accounts, saved content, or other tracking on this website.

4. Google Analytics

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 (property ID: G-26WJ4LRG87). Google Analytics collects anonymized data about how visitors use our site. We have enabled IP anonymization and do not pass any personally identifiable information to Google Analytics. Google may process this data on servers in the United States and other countries, subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You may opt out at any time by declining cookies or using the Google Analytics opt-out add-on linked above.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. The only third-party data processor used on this marketing site is Google Analytics (with your consent). We do not share data with any other third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect this website and its infrastructure. If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability, contact us at infosec@vestlife.com.
