About ANYA

Built for the parent who carries everything.

ANYA preserves what you know about your child — in your words, under your control — so that knowledge survives you, and the people who come next don't have to start from nothing.

The Problem

Most of what a parent knows has nowhere to go.

Parents of children with intellectual and developmental disabilities — including profound autism — carry knowledge that exists nowhere else. They have written emergency protocols in their heads. They know what calms their child, what triggers a crisis, what sounds like "fine" but never is.

That knowledge is not in any file. It is not in the records. It lives only in the parent. And when that parent is no longer able to explain it — because of illness, incapacity, or death — the people who step in have to learn from scratch, at the worst possible moment.

ANYA was built to solve this problem. Not eventually. Now, while you can still tell it everything.

What ANYA Is

Continuity infrastructure for lifelong care.

ANYA is not a general-purpose AI. It is not a records system. It is not therapy. It is something more specific — and more necessary.

ANYA holds what you know about your child — in your words, with the insights only you have — so that knowledge is preserved, current, and ready for the people who will need it when you cannot explain it yourself.

From the ANYA design brief, Tenerra.ai

ANYA is built on a framework called the Human Continuity Operating System (HCOS) — a structure developed by Vest Life, Inc. for ensuring that what a parent knows remains clear, usable, and ready for the people who will need it. The HCOS treats parent knowledge not as an add-on, but as the primary infrastructure of a child's life.

You tell Anya. She asks follow-up questions. She holds it all — current, organized, and in your voice — so that what you know is never lost and never has to live only in your head.

What We Believe

Six principles that guide everything ANYA does.

01

Your child is not their diagnosis.

Records describe conditions. ANYA preserves a person — the full, complex, specific person only a parent knows.

02

Parent knowledge is the primary record.

What you know is not supplemental. It is the most accurate, most complete, most important information that exists about your child.

03

Continuity must be built before it's needed.

When a crisis happens is the wrong time to start. ANYA exists so the work is already done when it matters most.

04

Privacy is not a feature. It is a foundation.

What you share in ANYA stays under your control. It will never be used to train models, inform products, or improve anything other than Anya knowing your child better.

05

ANYA is not a substitute for professionals.

ANYA guides. She does not diagnose, prescribe, or give legal advice. When a question falls outside her scope, she says so — and points you toward the right kind of help.

06

This is infrastructure, not software.

ANYA is not an app you use occasionally. It is the system that ensures nothing important about your child is ever lost.

Who ANYA Is For

Not built for everyone. Built for families like yours.

ANYA is built for parents whose children have profound autism, IDD, or complex care needs — parents who have written their own ER protocols, who carry knowledge that exists nowhere else, and who know that when they are gone or unable to explain, everything they know about their child could be lost.

ANYA is not built for families navigating a 504 plan. It is built for families whose children require lifelong, 24/7, high-complexity care from the people responsible for them — and for whom the question of what happens next is not abstract. It is urgent.

If you are that parent, ANYA was made for you.

The Founder

Built by someone who has spent 30 years in IDD law.

Michael Pearce

Founder, Vest Life, Inc. & Tenerra.ai

Attorney with 30 years of practice in intellectual and developmental disabilities law. Michael has spent his career at the intersection of law, family continuity, and the needs of children who cannot advocate for themselves. ANYA is the culmination of that work.

After three decades of representing families in IDD law, Michael Pearce watched the same crisis repeat: a parent becomes unable to explain what they know, and the people who take over start from zero — guessing, missing things, causing harm through ignorance rather than indifference.

ANYA was founded on a single conviction: the knowledge a parent holds is the most valuable, most fragile, and most overlooked asset in a profoundly disabled child's life. When a parent can no longer be there to explain it, that knowledge should already be preserved — accurate, accessible, and ready.

Vest Life, Inc. developed the Human Continuity Operating System as the framework. ANYA, at anya.tenerra.ai, is the implementation of that framework — built specifically for the families who need it most.
