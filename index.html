What is Anya?

Anya is a personal knowledge bridge for special needs parents — including parents of children with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD). It helps these parents capture their lived knowledge and organize it for their child's lifetime well-being continuity.

This system is designed for parents managing autism, developmental disabilities, and complex care needs. Anya supports continuity of well-being, coordination of supports, and knowledge transition across all ages and stages of life.

Unlike tools focused on the child, Anya is built for the parent. It ensures that what a parent knows about their child does not get lost over time.
