What is Anya?

ANYA turns what you know about your child into something others can understand and step into without losing what matters.

Anya is a personal knowledge bridge for special needs parents, including parents of children with intellectual and developmental disabilities, autism, developmental disabilities, and complex care needs.

It helps parents capture routines, preferences, support needs, communication patterns, behaviors, records, and lived knowledge so that nothing important gets lost when someone else needs to step in.

Anya functions as a living record rather than a static file. Every conversation can update the profile and make it more useful for caregiver handoffs, introductions to teachers or doctors, emergency context sharing, and long-term continuity planning.
